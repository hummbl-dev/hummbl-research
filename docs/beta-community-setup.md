# HUMMBL Beta Community Setup Guide

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** December 13, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 13, 2025

## Overview

The HUMMBL Beta Community serves as the central hub for beta participants to connect, share experiences, get support, and provide feedback. This guide outlines the setup and management of community platforms.

## Community Platforms

### Primary Platform: Discord Server

**Server Name:** HUMMBL Beta Community
**Invite Link:** https://discord.gg/hummbl-beta
**Purpose:** Real-time communication, support, and networking

#### Channel Structure

**Information Channels** (Read-only)
- `#announcements` - Official updates and news
- `#changelog` - Weekly feature updates and bug fixes
- `#roadmap` - Public roadmap and upcoming features
- `#resources` - Links to documentation and tutorials

**Support Channels**
- `#help-desk` - General support questions
- `#api-support` - API integration help
- `#bug-reports` - Bug reporting and tracking
- `#feature-requests` - New feature suggestions

**Discussion Channels**
- `#general` - Off-topic and casual conversation
- `#showcase` - Share your HUMMBL implementations
- `#architecture-discussion` - Technical architecture talks
- `#mental-models` - Framework and model discussions

**Special Interest Channels**
- `#beginners` - New user help and onboarding
- `#power-users` - Advanced features and tips
- `#integrations` - Third-party integrations
- `#academic` - Research and academic applications

**Voice Channels**
- `General Voice` - Casual voice chat
- `Office Hours` - Weekly live support sessions
- `Pair Programming` - Collaborative coding sessions

## Community Guidelines

### Code of Conduct

**Be Respectful**
- Treat all community members with respect and courtesy
- No harassment, discrimination, or offensive language
- Respect differing opinions and experiences

**Be Constructive**
- Provide helpful, actionable feedback
- Focus on problems, not people
- Share knowledge and help others learn

**Be Professional**
- Maintain professional communication standards
- Respect confidentiality of beta features
- Follow platform-specific rules

**Be Engaged**
- Participate actively in discussions
- Share your experiences and learnings
- Help maintain a positive community atmosphere

### Content Guidelines

**Allowed Content**
- Technical discussions and questions
- Bug reports and feature requests
- Code examples and implementations
- Case studies and success stories
- General beta experience sharing

**Prohibited Content**
- Spam or excessive self-promotion
- Off-topic advertising
- Sharing confidential beta information externally
- Harassment or discriminatory content
- Illegal or unethical activities

### Moderation Policy

**Warning System**
- **First Offense:** Private warning from moderator
- **Second Offense:** Public warning in channel
- **Third Offense:** Temporary ban (24-72 hours)
- **Repeated Offenses:** Permanent ban

**Moderation Tools**
- Message deletion for violations
- Temporary mutes for disruptive behavior
- Channel-specific bans when needed
- Permanent bans for severe violations

## Community Management

### Team Roles

**Community Manager**
- Overall community strategy and growth
- Content moderation and guideline enforcement
- Event organization and engagement
- Stakeholder communication

**Moderators (4-6 volunteers from beta users)**
- Daily moderation and support
- Content monitoring and cleanup
- User support and conflict resolution
- Community engagement activities

**Technical Support Liaisons**
- Bridge between community and engineering
- Technical question answering
- Bug report triage and follow-up
- Feature request collection

### Daily Operations

**Morning Check-in (9 AM EST)**
- Review overnight activity and issues
- Address urgent support requests
- Plan daily engagement activities

**Community Monitoring**
- Continuous monitoring during active hours
- Quick response to support questions (< 30 minutes)
- Content moderation and guideline enforcement

**Evening Roundup (6 PM EST)**
- Summarize daily activity and highlights
- Address any unresolved issues
- Plan next day's activities

### Weekly Activities

**Monday: Weekly Update**
- Share weekly changelog and roadmap updates
- Highlight community achievements
- Preview upcoming features

**Wednesday: Office Hours**
- Live Q&A session with product team
- Technical deep-dive discussions
- Community feedback collection

**Friday: Showcase Friday**
- Community member project showcases
- Success story sharing
- Weekend discussion prompts

### Monthly Activities

**Beta Roundtable**
- Monthly virtual meetup
- Guest speakers from HUMMBL team
- Community networking and collaboration

**Feedback Summit**
- Monthly feedback review session
- Roadmap voting and prioritization
- Strategic direction discussions

**Automated Welcome**
- Welcome message with key resources

## Crisis Management

### Platform Issues
- **Detection:** Monitoring alerts and user reports
- **Communication:** Immediate status updates
- **Alternative Channels:** Activate backup communication
- **Resolution:** Keep community informed throughout

### Community Conflicts
- **Early Intervention:** Private moderator discussion
- **Mediation:** Facilitated conversation between parties
- **Escalation:** Temporary restrictions if needed
- **Prevention:** Clear guidelines and regular reminders

### Negative Feedback
- **Acknowledgment:** Public recognition of concerns
- **Investigation:** Internal review and analysis
- **Response:** Transparent communication of actions
- **Follow-up:** Progress updates and resolution confirmation
