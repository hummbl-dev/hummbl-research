# IN12 - Risk Inversion

> Identify risks by assuming they are certainties and working backwards.

## Description

Risk Inversion flips probability by treating risks as inevitable, forcing detailed contingency planning and prevention strategies.

## Example

Cybersecurity: Assume "breach occurs"—what data is lost? This drives encryption and backups before attacks.
