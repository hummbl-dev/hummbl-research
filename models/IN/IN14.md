# IN14 - Assumption Inversion

> Challenge core assumptions by assuming they are false.

## Description

Assumption Inversion tests beliefs by negating them, revealing hidden dependencies and alternative paths.

## Example

Business plan: Assume "Market won't grow"—what then? This uncovers diversification needs.
