# P13 - Long-Term vs Short-Term

> Balance immediate gains against future consequences.

## Description

Long-Term vs Short-Term reframes choices by weighing temporal trade-offs, prioritizing sustainability over quick wins.

## Example

Sustainability: Short-term cost-cutting vs. long-term brand damage from poor practices. Choose eco-friendly options for enduring value.

- P03 – Second-Order Thinking (anticipates long-term effects)
- P08 – Future Backwards (starts from long-term goals)
