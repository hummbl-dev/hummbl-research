# P14 - Ethical Framing

> Evaluate decisions through moral and societal lenses.

## Description

Ethical Framing reframes problems by considering fairness, harm, and societal impact, ensuring responsible outcomes.

## Example

AI development: Beyond profit, frame as "Does this reduce bias or increase inequality?" Guides ethical design choices.
