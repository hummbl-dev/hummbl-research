# RE12 - Self-Referential Systems

> Systems that modify themselves based on their own outputs.

## Description

Self-Referential Systems apply recursion where the system reflects on and alters its own behavior, enabling adaptation.

## Example

AI learning: Model trains on its predictions, recursively improving accuracy.
