# SY02 - Pattern Recognition

> Identify recurring themes across disparate data.

## Description

Pattern Recognition synthesizes information by finding commonalities and trends. It connects dots to reveal underlying structures, enabling prediction and generalization.

## Example

In market analysis, recognize patterns like "tech bubbles follow hype cycles." Synthesis predicts downturns based on historical parallels.
