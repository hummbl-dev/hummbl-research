# SY04 - Meta-Analysis

> Analyze analyses to find higher-level truths.

## Description

Meta-Analysis synthesizes multiple studies or models to draw overarching conclusions. It accounts for biases and variability, providing robust insights from aggregated data.

## Example

In research, meta-analyze clinical trials to confirm drug efficacy. Synthesis reveals trends stronger than individual studies.
