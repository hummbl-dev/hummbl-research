# SY05 - Convergence Thinking

> Find where diverse paths lead to the same outcome.

## Description

Convergence Thinking synthesizes multiple approaches to identify common endpoints. It reveals universal principles or solutions that emerge regardless of starting points.

## Example

In innovation, different teams converge on similar solutions (e.g., touchscreens). Synthesis shows market needs drive convergence.
