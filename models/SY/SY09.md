# SY09 - Insight Synthesis

> Combine disparate insights into actionable wisdom.

## Description

Insight Synthesis weaves individual findings into overarching narratives, revealing hidden connections and strategic implications.

## Example

From market research: Synthesize "tech-savvy youth" + "rising sustainability" into "eco-tech products for Gen Z."
