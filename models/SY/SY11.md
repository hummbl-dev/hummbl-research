# SY11 - Abductive Reasoning

> Form the best explanation for observed facts.

## Description

Abductive Reasoning synthesizes hypotheses from evidence, selecting the most likely explanation among possibilities.

## Example

Mystery symptom: Fever + rash. Abduce "infection" over "allergy" based on patterns.

- SY07 – Theory Building (forms explanations)
- IN01 – Premortem Analysis (tests hypotheses)
