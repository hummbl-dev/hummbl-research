# SY12 - Integrative Thinking

> Resolve tensions by creating new options that incorporate opposing ideas.

## Description

Integrative Thinking synthesizes conflicting perspectives into superior solutions, avoiding compromise.

## Example

Cost vs. quality: Instead of cheap/low-quality, integrate "premium affordable" through innovation.
