# SY20 - Wisdom Synthesis

> Transforming knowledge into practical wisdom through integrated understanding.

## Description

Wisdom Synthesis is a mental model for converting accumulated knowledge into actionable wisdom. It goes beyond mere information synthesis to create deep, contextual understanding that enables effective judgment and decision-making in complex situations.

## Example

Medical diagnosis wisdom:

- Knowledge: Symptoms, test results, treatment options
- Information synthesis: Differential diagnosis
- Wisdom synthesis: Understanding patient context, values, and life circumstances
- Result: Treatment plan that balances medical effectiveness with patient quality of life

This transforms expert knowledge into compassionate, effective practice.
