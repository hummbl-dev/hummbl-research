# IN15 - Outcome Inversion

> Start from undesired outcomes and prevent them.

## Description

Outcome Inversion focuses on avoiding failures by defining bad results and engineering away from them.

## Example

Product safety: Define "user injury" as inverted outcome, then design guards and warnings to prevent it.

## Related Models

- IN01 – Premortem Analysis (starts from failure outcomes)
- IN10 – Antifragility Assessment (turns negatives into positives)
