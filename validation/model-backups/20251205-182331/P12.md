# P12 - Stakeholder Mapping

> Identify and prioritize all parties affected by decisions.

## Description

Stakeholder Mapping reframes problems by visualizing interests, influence, and impacts of involved parties, ensuring balanced and inclusive solutions.

## Example

Product launch: Map customers (high interest), competitors (high influence), regulators (low interest). Tailor strategy accordingly.
