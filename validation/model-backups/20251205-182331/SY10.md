# SY10 - Grand Unification

> Seek unifying principles that explain diverse phenomena.

## Description

Grand Unification synthesizes fragmented knowledge into universal laws or frameworks, pursuing elegance and simplicity in complexity.

## Example

Physics: Unify electromagnetism and weak force into electroweak theory, explaining particle interactions.
